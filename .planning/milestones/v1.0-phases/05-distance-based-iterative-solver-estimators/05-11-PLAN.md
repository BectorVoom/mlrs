Purpose: the per-PRD memory-gate reconciliation (D-10 + D-04) — locks in that the iterative solvers and DBSCAN stay bounded/buffer-reusing even though they read back (the visible departure from the strict device-resident pipeline).
Output: two new gate functions + doc comments in `memory_gate_test.rs`.

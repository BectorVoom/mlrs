<objective>
Land LogisticRegression (LINEAR-05) — the project's highest-risk estimator — on the validated L-BFGS primitive (05-06). `LogisticRegression<F>` implements `Fit` (L-BFGS minimize over the symmetric multinomial softmax objective, D-12 — binary is the 2-class case of the same path), `PredictLabels` (argmax of predict_proba), and `PredictProba` (stable softmax of the fitted logits). Pinned: objective `(1/n)·Σloss + ½·l2_reg·‖coef‖²`, `l2_reg = 1/(C·n)`, intercept UNPENALIZED (Pitfall 3); L-BFGS-B `m=10`/`gtol=1e-4`/`ftol=64·eps`/`maxiter=100`. The oracle gates on `predict_proba`/`predict` (gauge-invariant under the symmetric over-parameterization) at 1e-5 as PRIMARY, with `coef_` at a documented LOOSER per-family bound as the secondary check (Pitfall 5 / VALIDATION gauge-freedom escape hatch).

Purpose: LINEAR-05 — the L-BFGS iterative-solver classifier.
Output: `linear/logistic.rs`, `linear/mod.rs` (mod line), `logistic_test.rs` (binary + multiclass).
</objective>
